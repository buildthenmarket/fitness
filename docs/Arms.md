# Arms

## Biceps

The biceps has three main components:

- Brachialis
- Short head
- Long head

## Triceps

The triceps has three heads:
- Long head
- Lateral head
- Medial head

---

## Calisthenics

<details>
<summary>Bodyweight arm exercises</summary>

### Biceps

- Chin-ups (supinated grip emphasizes biceps)
- Inverted rows (underhand grip)

### Triceps

- Diamond push-ups
- Close-grip push-ups
- Bench dips

</details>

## Dumbbell

<details>
<summary>Dumbbell arm exercises</summary>

### Biceps Curls

- Standard biceps curl
- [Zottman curl](https://www.youtube.com/shorts/uYWqEEaEj6A) (palms up on curl, rotate to palms down on lower)
- Close-Grip Hammer Curl (both arms same time, holding dumbbells together vertically)
- Hammer curl (neutral grip)
- Incline dumbbell curl
- Concentration curl
- Preacher curl with dumbbell

### Triceps

#### Overhead Triceps Extension

[![Overhead triceps extension](https://img.youtube.com/vi/BrVvtti-gkU/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=shorts/BrVvtti-gkU)

- Single arm overhead extension
- Two arm overhead extension
- Dumbbell skull crushers
- Tricep kickbacks

</details>
