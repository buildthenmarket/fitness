# Dumbbell Wrist exercises

- Farmer carries, wrist curls

<details>
<summary>Dumbbell grip exercises</summary>

### Farmer Carry Hold

With a kettlebell or dumbbell in each hand, stand with perfect posture with the weights in the farmer carry position for 1 minute.

- Work up to 3 sets of 1 minute as heavy as possible.

### Dumbbell Head Hold

Place two DBs on their ends and pick them up by their head. Hold a farmer carry position this way.

- Work up to 3 sets of 30 seconds to one minute hold.

![Dumbbell Head Hold](https://breakingmuscle.com//wp-content/uploads/2019/09/dumbbellhold.jpeg "Dumbbell Head Hold")

### Wrist Curls

- Palms up wrist curls (forearm flexors)
- Palms down wrist curls (forearm extensors)
- Behind the back wrist curls

### Farmer's Walk

Walk with heavy dumbbells at your sides, maintaining posture and grip.

## Gym / Weight Room

<details>
<summary>Plate and machine grip exercises</summary>

### Plate Pinch Hold or Walk

Hold on to 15-25lb plates by pinching them together, or even two if you can manage it. You can also do these as a walking plate pinch.

- Work up to 3 sets of 1 minute or 3 sets of a 40-60 meter walk.

![Plate Pinch Hold](https://breakingmuscle.com//wp-content/uploads/2019/09/platehold.jpeg "Plate Pinch Hold")

### Barbell Wrist Curls

- Seated barbell wrist curls
- Reverse barbell wrist curls

### Cable Wrist Curls

- Cable wrist curls with straight bar attachment

### Fat Gripz

Add fat grips to any barbell or dumbbell exercise to increase grip demand.

</details>
